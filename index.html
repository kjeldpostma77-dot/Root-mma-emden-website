Who are we?

We are a martial arts club that want to bring high level martial arts training to Emden. We teach the foundational arts of fighting: Grappling (brazilian jiu jitsu), wrestling, boxing and kickboxing. We also teach the style together with mma (mixed martial arts).

Aside from teaching adults we have a heavy emphasis on kids training. We think that teaching kids martial arts at a young age can be very benificial for their development in teaching respect, self esteem and body control. We look forward to welcoming you on our mats!
